What's the CyberEdu Game?

Playing as a college student immersed in the cyber world, students experience several scenes that teach cybersecurity topics relevant today. With the game's modules and accompanied videos, players gain practical knowledge about items such as safe downloads, Wi-Fi security, app permissions, and spear phishing. All while in a virtual gaming environment with a compelling storyline.

Developed by students at University of Maryland, College Park, the CyberEdu Game is an interactive game that teaches students everyday concepts in cybersecurity.

About

What is CyberEdu?

An educational platform for users to develop basic cyber security skills through a virtual game and interactive exercises.

It was started by a group of students at University of Maryland College Park: Bobby Crumbaugh, Jonathan Hansford, Corbin Ford, and Jules Iglesia.

Why CyberEdu?

We built CyberEdu to help others stay safe while using their computer. By informing users on the potential risks and how one can mitigate them, we hope our platform will reduce the number of cyber victims.

Who is this for?

Anyone that uses a computer in their day-to-day tasks! Check out the video below.
